Survive Space (Alpha Release)

An unfinished game by Eamonn McHugh-Roohr

WASD/Arrows to move
E to interact
M to bring up map
TAB to open crafting menu
Once you've crafted and equipped a weapon, SPACE to fire
If you die you will respawn in a shuttle
Explore the procgen galaxy

Normal maps generated with: https://azagaya.itch.io/laigter
